About Me

Last year I quit my job and bought a one way ticket to Europe. I travelled for a month then found a job as a bartender in Cologne. I also worked as a walking tour guide which allowed me to meet lots of people. While I was living there I was able to travel to a few other countries as well. I recently moved back to Canada to work as a field supervisor for a six month contract. I will be going back to Europe in January on a way ticket again and going to travel as much as I can.

I have been teaching myself software development through different resources. I have built this site myself with html, css, bootstrap and javascript. I am hoping to make this a career I can do remote to continue to travel.
