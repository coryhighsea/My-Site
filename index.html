Welcome to my Site

I have created this website to highlight my travels while showcasing the web development skills I have aquired. I lived in Germany for 8 months and visited a few other countries while I lived there. I have some blog posts and photos from these travels. I also have blog posts on healthy living or other parts of my life. I plan on to continue to travel in the future. Part of the focus of this site is to practice the web development skills I am learning. I am constantly updating the site with new features as I learn them. I find it a good supplement to my learning to have a real project.

On the blog post page you can find a few small blogs as I mentioned earlier. There are pictures in this section as well. The next page is a Cities I've Visited page Where I used leaflet.js library to create an interactive map where I put pins of what cities I have visited. Most of them are in Europe. On the next page I have created a little photo Gallery with some cool CSS features I have been working on. The pictures aren't half bad either. I am thinking of investing in a decent camera and to create some quality photos. But for now the presentation is where the quality is coming from. Finally there is a contact page where you can find links to my social media and my email.

If a site like this or any of the features on this site are of interest to you, please let me know. I would be happy to help in creating a site or an app for you. I am experienting in data visualization libraries that can create interactive graphs and charts to display data. I believe these features will go well along with leaflet mapping. I can even help with making a WordPress page as well. That route is good for the end user to be able to update their site.
